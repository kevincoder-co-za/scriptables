Conclusion & Roadmap

Thank you for using Scriptables. My goal was to make server management as painless as possible, especially for busy developers who don't have time to fiddle with infrastructure.
I appreciate your support, and hope that I have achieved this goal in making your server management suck so much less, and save you a ton of time.
Here is our development roadmap for the next 8 months:
  1. 1.
    Backing up to S3 and disk ~ November 2023
  2. 2.
    Wordpress & Symfony support ~ November 2023
  3. 3.
    Laravel Horizon and queues setup ~ November 2023
  4. 4.
    Load balancing ~ January 2023
  5. 5.
    Docker container orchestration ~ April 2024
  6. 6.
    "Task Manager" like interface, where you can view disk space usage, CPU, RAM and other stats. ~ April 2024
  7. 7.
    Django, Next, Nuxt and other JavaScript frameworks support ~ April 2024
  8. 8.
    Monitoring service - including SSL certificate checks, CRON job checks, event monitoring similar to cloudwatch and more. ~ April 2024
  9. 9.
    Customer suggested features ~ we will put these up here as they come in.
